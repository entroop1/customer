We evaluated around 10 different beacon manufacturers before selecting Kontakt.io. The beacons had to be small and discrete…

We evaluated around 10 different beacon manufacturers before selecting Kontakt.io. The beacons had to be small and discrete but also have a replaceable battery. Kontakt.io’s beacons covered both of these requirements. In addition, the beacons can be put into sleep mode during closing hours each day in order to extend battery life, this is important to us as the size of the network makes swapping out batteries an onerous task. What we really appreciate is that Kontakt.io prints the unique identification number on the beacon casing. That is one thing a user might not consider in the beginning of rolling out a network, but after installing around 150 beacons, being able to perform a visual check of each new beacon’s number rather than having to scan it with a Bluetooth device saves time and aggravation—especially when installing a beacon near another could be accidentally picked up by a reader.

Markus Bokowsky
